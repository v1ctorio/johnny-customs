# How to pay

There are two ways to pay the customs charges. Note that information here may not be accurate, so check the official Correos links for more up-to-date information.

### 1. Online

You can pay the customs charges online by going to the [Correos Aduanas website](https://www.correosaduanas.es).  
Enter the tracking number of your package and the amount of the customs charges will be shown.

### 2. Post office

Most of the times you will get a letter demanding the payment of the customs charges.
To pay them, you will need to go to the Correos office where your package is being held. You will need to bring and identification document with picture (pasaporte, DNI, permiso de residencia) and the .
